6th Annual Collin College Guitar Festival

Guest Artists: Quattro Palos

February 9-11, 2018

Contact: Dr. Fernand Vera

The Sixth Annual Guitar Festival will be held the weekend of February 9-11, 2018, with guest artists Quattro Palos. The event annoucement handbill can be downloaded HERE

The festival kicks off at 4:30 p.m. on Friday, February 9th at the Spring Creek Campus, located at 2800 E. Spring Creek Parkway in Plano.

The festival will include by faculty, the quest artists, alumni and attendees. Plus, master and technique classes.

The festival is open to guitarists of all ages and levels.

The event is free for Collin College students, but a donation of $25 is suggested. Otherwise, tuition ranges between $60-100. The application and full event schedule can be downloaded HERE

For more information, contact Dr. Fernand Vera at fvera@collin.edu or 972.881.5613.
