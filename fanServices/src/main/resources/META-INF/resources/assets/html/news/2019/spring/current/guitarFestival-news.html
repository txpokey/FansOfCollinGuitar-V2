7th Annual Collin College Guitar Festival

February 8-10, 2019

Dr. Robby Gibson and Solazur

Artistic Directors: Drs. Olga Amelkina-Vera and Fernand Vera

Contact: Dr. Fernand Vera

The Seventh Annual Guitar Festival will be held the weekend of February 8-10, 2019, with guest artists Dr. Robby Gibson and Solazur (Collin Alums Russ Callison and Katie Burns). The event is FREE. For an example of a past festival's student ensemble performance, you can view several on You Tube, starting at this location. Please fill out the event registration form, located here. An event schedule and annoucement handbill can be downloaded HERE The festival's student ensemble music can be downloaded from HERE, so students can start on their parts as early as they wish.

The festival kicks off at 4:30 p.m. on Friday, February 8th at the Spring Creek Campus, located at 2800 E. Spring Creek Parkway in Plano.

The festival will include by faculty, the quest artists, alumni and attendees. Plus, master and technique classes.

The festival is open to guitarists of all ages and levels.

The event is free.

For more information, contact Dr. Fernand Vera at fvera@collin.edu or 972.881.5613.
